About CitizenAI

CitizenAI is a government-centric AI solution designed to enhance communication between citizens and public service departments. Our platform leverages cutting-edge natural language processing models to provide real-time support, analyze public sentiment, and collect feedback from the community.

What We Do

  • Answer citizen questions using AI
  • Analyze feedback to detect sentiment (positive, negative, neutral)
  • Enable users to report concerns and issues easily
  • Provide insights for improving public services

Our Mission

At CitizenAI, our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities by creating transparent, accessible, and intelligent digital governance tools.

Technologies Used

  • AI Models: Falcon, Granite (IBM), Hugging Face Transformers
  • Web Framework: Flask (Python)
  • Frontend: HTML, Jinja2 Templates, CSS

Who Can Use It?

CitizenAI is ideal for municipalities, e-governance platforms, and public institutions seeking to modernize their interaction with the public and drive data-informed decision-making.

Get Involved

We're constantly improving CitizenAI. Whether you're a developer, policymaker, or citizen advocate, we welcome contributions and feedback to help shape the future of AI in governance.
